## Partnership Economics

### Revenue Sharing Model

**Tier 1 Partners (Strategic):**
- Revenue share: 20-30%
- Minimum commitment: $100K+ annual
- Dedicated account management
- Co-marketing budget

**Tier 2 Partners (Growth):**
- Revenue share: 15-20%
- Minimum commitment: $25K+ annual
- Account management
- Marketing support

**Tier 3 Partners (Affiliate):**
- Revenue share: 10-15%
- No minimum commitment
- Self-service integration
- Limited support

### Partner Incentive Structure

**Volume Bonuses:**
- 5% bonus at $500K annual revenue
- 10% bonus at $1M annual revenue
- 15% bonus at $5M annual revenue

**Performance Bonuses:**
- User acquisition bonuses
- Retention bonuses
- Engagement bonuses

**Equity Opportunities:**
- Strategic partners may receive equity
- Vesting over 4 years
- Board representation for major partners
