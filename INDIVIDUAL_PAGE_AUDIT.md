- **Required:** Dedicated guarantee section with:

  - Visual guarantee badge

- **Required:** Add ethical urgency:
  - "Limited spots available this month (12 remaining)"
  - "Price increases to $149 on Feb 1st"
  - "First 100 members get lifetime pricing lock"

### 7. **Unclear Value Proposition**
- **Current:** "Transform stress into strength"
- **Required:** Specific, measurable outcome:
  - "Reduce anxiety by 60% in 30 days (or your money back)"
  - "Stop panic attacks in 21 days with our proven system"
  - "Sleep through the night again‚Äîstarting tonight"

### 8. **Missing "How It Works" Process Section**
- **Current:** Features listed, but no clear process
- **Required:** Simple 3-step process:
  1. **Book Your Call** - 15-min discovery call to assess your needs
  2. **Get Your Plan** - Personalized 30-day resilience roadmap
  3. **Transform** - Daily AI coaching + weekly check-ins = measurable results

### 9. **Weak CTA Optimization**
- **Current:** "Book Your Discovery Call" (good) and "Start Your Free Trial" (confusing - no trial mentioned)
- **Required:** Consistent, benefit-driven CTAs:
  - Primary: "Book Your Free Discovery Call" (emphasize FREE)
  - Secondary: "See Pricing & Plans"
  - Eliminate "Start Your Free Trial" unless offering actual trial

### 10. **Missing Comparison/Alternatives Section**
- **Current:** No competitive positioning

  - **Traditional Therapy:** $200/session, 6-month wait, weekly appointments
