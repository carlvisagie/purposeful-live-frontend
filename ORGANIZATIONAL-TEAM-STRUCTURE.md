# Purposeful Live Coaching - Organizational & Team Structure

**Version:** 1.0 | **Status:** Strategic Blueprint

## Executive Leadership

### CEO/Founder
- **Responsibility:** Overall vision, strategy, fundraising
- **Reports to:** Board of Directors
- **Direct reports:** CFO, CTO, COO, CMO

### Chief Technology Officer (CTO)
- **Responsibility:** Product, engineering, infrastructure
- **Team size:** 50-100 engineers (Year 3-5)
- **Key initiatives:** Platform development, AI/ML, integrations

### Chief Financial Officer (CFO)
- **Responsibility:** Finance, accounting, investor relations
- **Team size:** 5-10 people
- **Key initiatives:** Fundraising, financial planning, compliance

### Chief Operating Officer (COO)
- **Responsibility:** Operations, HR, legal, compliance
- **Team size:** 10-20 people
- **Key initiatives:** Scaling operations, compliance, partnerships

### Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)
- **Responsibility:** Marketing, brand, customer acquisition
- **Team size:** 15-30 people
- **Key initiatives:** Brand building, customer acquisition, partnerships

## Product & Engineering

### VP Product
- **Responsibility:** Product strategy, roadmap, requirements
- **Team size:** 3-5 product managers
- **Key initiatives:** Feature prioritization, user research, analytics

### VP Engineering
- **Responsibility:** Engineering execution, architecture, quality
- **Team size:** 40-80 engineers
- **Key initiatives:** Platform development, performance, reliability

### VP Data & AI
- **Responsibility:** Data strategy, ML models, analytics
- **Team size:** 10-20 data scientists and engineers
- **Key initiatives:** Personalization, predictions, insights

## Coaching & Content

### VP Coaching Operations
- **Responsibility:** Coach recruitment, training, quality
- **Team size:** 5-10 coaches coordinators
- **Key initiatives:** Coach management, quality assurance, training

### Director of Content
- **Responsibility:** Content strategy, creation, curation
- **Team size:** 5-10 content creators
- **Key initiatives:** Course creation, expert interviews, library expansion

## Finance & Operations

### Controller
- **Responsibility:** Accounting, financial reporting
- **Team size:** 3-5 accountants
- **Key initiatives:** Financial accuracy, compliance, reporting

### VP Human Resources
- **Responsibility:** Recruitment, culture, compensation

## Compensation & Equity

### Equity Structure
- Founder/CEO: 40%
- Early investors: 30%
- Employee pool: 20%
- Future investors: 10%

### Employee Equity
- Early employees (Year 1): 0.5-2%
- Mid-level employees: 0.1-0.5%
- New hires: 0.01-0.1%
- Vesting: 4 years with 1-year cliff

### Compensation Philosophy
- Competitive market rates
- Performance-based bonuses
- Equity incentives
- Benefits and perks
- Professional development

## Culture & Values

### Core Values
- **Excellence:** Highest standards in everything we do
- **Integrity:** Honest, ethical, transparent
- **Innovation:** Continuous improvement and learning
- **Impact:** Focused on health outcomes, not vanity metrics
- **Collaboration:** Teamwork and cross-functional alignment

### Culture Initiatives
- Remote-first culture (distributed team)
- Flexible work arrangements
- Professional development budget
- Annual team retreats
- Wellness programs for employees
- Diversity and inclusion initiatives
